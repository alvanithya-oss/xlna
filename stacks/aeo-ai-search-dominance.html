SEO is Becoming AEO

As Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and ChatGPT Search replace link-based ranking with answer-based synthesis, traditional SEO evolves into Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). This stack is a turnkey namespace for launching an AEO agency, SaaS platform, education hub, or analytics engine — capturing the fastest-growing digital marketing sub-discipline.
