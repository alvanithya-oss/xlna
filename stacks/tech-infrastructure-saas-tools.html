The Utility Layer of the Internet

While frontier technologies define future categories, recurring SaaS utilities power daily operations. CDN services, product demo intelligence, analytics tools, digital signatures, and security utilities represent modular, revenue-ready infrastructure. This stack is suited for individual product launches or aggregation under a unified SaaS holding brand.
