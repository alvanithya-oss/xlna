Market Thesis

Compliance is Recurring Revenue

Regulatory pressure continues to expand across fintech, healthcare, cybersecurity, and enterprise software. Compliance-as-a-Service (CaaS) and AI-powered legal automation are rapidly replacing manual review processes. This stack captures the plugin layer, automation layer, cybersecurity layer, and communication layer of legal-tech.
