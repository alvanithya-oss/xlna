// Common Questions

Acquisition FAQ

Answers to the questions institutional buyers ask before initiating a domain acquisition.

Can I acquire a single domain rather than a full stack?

Yes. Every stack can be acquired whole or fractionally. Individual anchor domains are available at standalone pricing. Transaction structures are flexible depending on the asset and strategic fit.

Are all domains .com extensions only?

Every domain across all eight stacks is a premium .com. We do not list alternative TLD extensions in our stacks. The sole exception is legalplugin.ai in Stack 07, included for its strategic pairing value with legalplugin.com.

How does the acquisition process work?

Submit an enquiry via the form on this page. We respond within 24 hours with a term sheet or counter-proposal. All transactions are supported via Escrow.com or Afternic.com. No brokers, no auctions, no waiting.

What is AEO and why do these domains command a premium?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the emerging discipline of optimizing for AI-driven answer systems — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE. Brands that own the namespace in this category gain lasting visibility advantages as AI-native search becomes primary. These domains are first-mover assets in a rapidly forming category.

Can stacks be split across multiple buyers?

Under certain conditions, yes. Non-competing buyers acquiring complementary domains within a stack can be accommodated. Anchor domains are typically sold exclusively to a single acquirer to preserve brand integrity.

What makes XLNA stacks different from broker listings?

These are not brokered listings or aftermarket pickups. Each stack was assembled with deliberate thesis — acquiring the semantic territory of a category before it reaches critical mass. Buyers receive strategic namespace depth, not isolated assets.
